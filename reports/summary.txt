ALUPA FOODS PRIVATE LIMITED, with its registered office at DHANYALAXMI RICE MILL, Puttur, Udupi, Karnataka, has formally appointed MALLYA & MALLYA as its statutory auditors. This appointment, confirmed during the Annual General Meeting on September 26, 2022, is set for a period commencing from April 2022 and concluding on March 31, 2027. The auditor's office is located at 29/2, 1st Floor, Parijatha Complex, Race Course Road, Bangalore, Karnataka. For further inquiries, the company can be contacted via mail@alupafoods.in, and the auditors at mallyaandmallya@gmail.com.
